## Vanta — Signal-Based Prospecting at Scale

**Company:** Vanta (trust management / compliance automation platform)
**Source:** https://www.clay.com/customers/vanta

### What They Built
- Replaced fragmented sales tech stack with Clay as the central signal orchestration engine
- Automated account research that reps previously did manually before every customer conversation

### Key Clay Workflows
1. **Inbound lead enrichment:** When a lead fills a form → HubSpot does initial enrichment → Clay runs comprehensive enrichment (job titles, company size, revenue, geo, email validation, phone numbers, LinkedIn cross-check)
2. **Signal-based outbound:** Clay monitors for:
   - Funding announcements (newly funded companies need compliance certs for enterprise deals)
   - Hiring patterns (companies hiring security engineers or compliance officers)
   - AI content analysis of job postings for SOC2-related language
   - Website changes related to compliance
   - CISO job postings
3. **Automated account research:** When a rep claims an account in Salesforce → Clay auto-researches security/compliance readiness indicators from company websites → summarizes findings in CRM

### Results
- 80%+ enrichment coverage by combining multiple data sources
- Thousands of new contacts added monthly through automated workflows
- Automated manual research tasks across all sales reps
- Real-time outreach based on funding events, hiring signals, and compliance updates

### Key Insight for Playbooks
Vanta layers MULTIPLE signals simultaneously (4+ signals at once) rather than relying on single triggers. This multi-signal stacking is the gold standard for GTM Alpha.

## Regency Supply / Coverflex — Automated Signal-Based Outreach for 3M+ Companies

**Company:** Coverflex (flexible benefits platform, operating in Portugal, Spain, Italy)
**Source:** https://www.clay.com/customers/coverflex

### What They Built
- Built an internal "ZoomInfo" using Clay + N8N + Postgres
- Monitors 3M+ companies monthly for buying signals
- Generates 200+ demos per month from automated signal-based outreach

### Key Clay Workflows
1. **Monthly signal sweep:** All 3M companies run through Clay checking for:
   - Headcount changes
   - Website visits
   - Job postings
   - LinkedIn engagement
   - Other custom signals
2. **Decision-maker identification:** Clay identifies key personas (HR, Finance, CEO) and enriches with contact details, LinkedIn URLs, mobile numbers, recent posts, job changes
3. **Multi-touch deployment:** Qualified accounts get:
   - Automated personalized email sequences (via Lemlist)
   - Custom presentations and product tours
   - Automated personalized handwritten letters via mail

### Key Insight for Playbooks
The Clay + N8N + database pattern (or Clay + CRM) enables always-on prospecting machines that scale to millions of accounts. This is the infrastructure model to recommend.

## Sendoso — $1M Pipeline from Creative Outbound

**Company:** Sendoso (direct mail + gifting platform)
**Source:** https://www.clay.com/blog/clay-sendoso-case-study

### What They Built
- Centralized outbound motion: contact enrichment, signal capture, data syncs, CRM lookups, personalized messaging all in Clay
- Went from 20 personalized emails/day to fully automated at scale

### Key Clay Workflows
1. **Automated personalized gifting:** Import contacts → Clay enriches with LinkedIn data, recent posts, job changes → AI crafts personalized gift messages → Sendoso API auto-sends tailored gifts

### Key Insight for Playbooks
The combination of signals + creative channels (not just email) is a powerful differentiator. Recommend multi-channel plays that combine email, LinkedIn, direct mail, and gifting where appropriate.

## Cross-Cutting Patterns to Reference in Playbooks

1. **Waterfall enrichment** — Always recommend combining multiple data providers (Clay's core strength). Single-provider enrichment leaves 20-40% of data gaps.
2. **Signal stacking** — Layer 2-4 signals before triggering outreach. Single signals produce noise; stacked signals produce intent.
3. **Experimentation velocity** — The winning GTM teams test more plays, faster. Clay enables 10x more experiments per quarter.
4. **Always-on monitoring** — Move from batch prospecting to continuous signal monitoring with Clay Signals + Custom Signals.
5. **Research automation** — Automate the manual research reps do before calls. This alone saves hours per rep per day.
6. **Multi-channel orchestration** — Don't just email. Use Clay data to power LinkedIn, direct mail, gifting, and ad targeting simultaneously.
