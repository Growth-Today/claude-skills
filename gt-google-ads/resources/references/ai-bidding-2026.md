# 2026 AI Bidding — Journey-Aware Bidding & Smart Bidding Exploration (B2B)

The 2026 Smart Bidding updates that matter most for B2B lead gen. Load alongside `bidding.md` and `crm-attribution.md`. **Evidence discipline:** vendor/Google performance figures are claims to validate.

## Journey-Aware Bidding (JAB) — the B2B one to care about
A Smart Bidding beta for **Search + Target CPA** that lets Google learn from the **full lead-to-sale journey in your CRM**, not just front-end form fills. It reads intermediate touchpoints — guide download, webinar attendance, demo booked, **MQL, SQL** — as *signals* to find clicks most likely to become pipeline, **without those events having to be the bidding target**.

- **Why it's big for B2B:** long, multi-touch cycles are exactly where front-end-only bidding misfires. Advertisers with lead-volume or lead-*quality* problems benefit most.
- **Prerequisite:** you must be importing **offline conversions** (see `crm-attribution.md`) and pushing them **tighter than weekly batches** — JAB is only as good as the CRM signal you feed it. No offline data → no journey to be aware of.
- **How to adopt:** get ECL/OCI + value clean first, then enable JAB on a tCPA Search campaign and let it learn (~2–4 weeks).

## Smart Bidding Exploration
Expands the auction the bidder explores; now extending into **PMax + Shopping** (Shopping = out of scope for B2B). A **demand-led pacing** model shifts daily spend toward higher-predicted-demand periods within the same monthly budget. Reported: **+27% unique converting users** on Search on average.
- **B2B use:** useful on high-intent Search once tracking + offline conversions are solid; watch **lead quality**, not just converting-user volume — exploration can widen into lower-intent queries.

## AI Max for Search (context)
AI Max broadens matching + creative with AI. For B2B it is **only safe on clean offline-conversion signal + value-based bidding** — otherwise it optimizes to cheap junk leads. Pair AI Max/Broad Match with ECL + JAB, tight negatives, and pipeline reporting.

**Common thread:** all three 2026 levers assume you feed Google **real pipeline signal**. Fix CRM attribution first (`crm-attribution.md`); then AI bidding compounds it.
