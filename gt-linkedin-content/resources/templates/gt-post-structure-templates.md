## Template A: The System Reveal (2,000-2,500 chars)

**Best for:** Clay workflows, automation builds, campaign systems, AI agent reveals
**Funnel:** MoF · **Formula:** System Reveal · **C/R target:** 0.48+

```
[HOOK: Bold result + timeframe. Max 15 words.]

[1-line credibility: "We run this for [X]+ B2B GTM clients."]

Here's the exact system:

Step 1: [Action label]
↳ [Specific detail — tool, method, output]
↳ [What happens next]

Step 2: [Action label]
↳ [Specific detail — tool, method, output]
↳ [What happens next]

Step 3: [Action label]
↳ [Specific detail — tool, method, output]
↳ [Result of this step]

[Optional Step 4-5 for complex systems]

The result: [Specific metric — meetings, leads, hours saved, reply rate]

[CTA: "Comment '[KEYWORD]' and I'll send the template." or "Over to you: what's your current [X] setup?"]

P.S. [Resource offer or service signal]
```

**Character target:** 1,800-2,500
**Media:** Image (workflow diagram or annotated screenshot) — 2.3x reach multiplier
**Single image alternative:** dense infographic, one clear structure, designed in Claude Design

## Template B: The Journey Post / How It Started (1,500-2,000 chars)

**Best for:** Agency building, client wins, personal milestones, building in public
**Funnel:** MoF/BoF · **Formula:** Journey Post · **C/R target:** high quality comments

```
How it started: [Specific bad/humble state with number]
How it's going: [Specific impressive result with number]

[1-2 sentences of honest context — what changed]

Here's what made the difference:

1. [Lesson or milestone]
↳ [1-2 sentence explanation]

2. [Lesson or milestone]
↳ [1-2 sentence explanation]

3. [Lesson or milestone]
↳ [1-2 sentence explanation]

[Optional: 4-5 for longer posts]

[Closing sentence: forward-looking or grateful, not a CTA]

P.S. [Service signal or resource offer]
```

**Character target:** 1,200-2,000
**Media:** Text-only acceptable for personal stories · Proof visual if results-heavy
**Note:** This is the highest comment-quality format. Personal vulnerability + specific results = trust.

## Template C: The Contrarian Take (1,200-1,800 chars)

**Best for:** Hot takes, "X is dead", industry myths, thought leadership
**Funnel:** MoF · **Formula:** Contrarian Take · **C/R target:** debate-driven

```
[HOOK: "[Common belief] is [wrong/dead/overrated]." — max 10 words]

[Acknowledge the common view — 1-2 sentences]

Here's what's actually happening:

↳ [Specific evidence from our experience — with number]
↳ [Second piece of evidence]
↳ [Third piece of evidence]

Here's what actually works instead:

↳ [Alternative approach 1]
↳ [Alternative approach 2]
↳ [Alternative approach 3]

[Proof point: "We've seen this across [X]+ B2B clients."]

Over to you: [Opinion question — NOT "Agree?"]

P.S. [Follow CTA or resource]
```

**Character target:** 1,000-1,800
**Media:** Comparison visual ("How X is viewed: by most people vs GTM engineers") — GT brand styling, designed in Claude Design
**Note:** Body must deliver the evidence the hook promises. Never bait and disappoint.

## Template D: The Resource Drop / Comment-Gate (1,000-1,500 chars)

**Best for:** Lead magnets, template giveaways, trigger lists, playbook drops
**Funnel:** BoF · **Formula:** Resource Drop · **Avg comments:** 267 (2.7x baseline)

```
[HOOK: Bold claim + specific number. "37 of the best..." or "We've tested X and found..."]

[2-3 lines: why this matters to your reader right now]

Here's what's inside:

↳ [Teaser item 1 — specific, not vague]
↳ [Teaser item 2]
↳ [Teaser item 3]
↳ [Bonus item or escalation]

Comment "[KEYWORD]" and I'll send it over.
Make sure we're connected so I can DM you.

P.S. [Service signal — "We build these systems for B2B GTM teams at Growth Today. DM me 'GTM' to learn more."]
```

**Character target:** 800-1,500
**Media:** List graphic — clean typography, numbered items, brand colors
**Note:** Only use this format when you have a real deliverable to send. Never comment-gate without the resource.

## Template E: The Tool Comparison / Stack Reveal (1,800-2,500 chars)

**Best for:** Tech stack reveals, head-to-head tool comparisons, "every tool we use"

```
[1-line context: "Here's what we actually found after running this across [X]+ clients."]

[Tool/Category 1]:
↳ [What it does — 1 line]
↳ [Our verdict — specific, not vague]

[Tool/Category 2]:
↳ [What it does — 1 line]
↳ [Our verdict]

[Tool/Category 3]:
↳ [What it does — 1 line]
↳ [Our verdict]

[Continue for 4-7 tools depending on post type]

Overall winner for [use case]: [Pick one — be decisive]

Over to you: [question about their current stack]

P.S. [Follow CTA or resource offer — "We break down our full Clay + Claude stack every week. Follow Growth Today."]
```

**Character target:** 1,500-2,500
**Media:** Single image tool map (one dense image, tool logos) OR list graphic with tool logos — designed in Claude Design

## Template F: The Trend Explainer (1,200-1,800 chars)

**Best for:** AI/tech news with GTM angle, "what X means for your outbound", new model releases
**Funnel:** ToF · **Formula:** Trend Explainer · **Reactions target:** 1,000+

```
[HOOK: Trending topic name OR "Everyone's talking about [X]." OR "[X] just changed [Y]."]

[1-line promise: "Here's what it means for your GTM in 60 seconds."]

[Visual required — diagram, architecture chart, or explainer graphic]

What it is:
↳ [Simple 1-line definition]

Why it matters for GTM:
↳ [Specific GTM application 1]
↳ [Specific GTM application 2]
↳ [Specific GTM application 3]

What most people are missing:
↳ [The GTM-specific insight that's not obvious]

[Soft closer — no hard CTA for ToF posts]

P.S. [Follow CTA: "I translate AI trends into GTM plays every week. Follow Growth Today."]
```

**Character target:** 1,000-1,800
**Media:** Visual is mandatory for ToF — diagram, infographic, or architecture image. Text-only ToF underperforms.
**Note:** This is a reach play, not a pipeline play. Don't add a resource CTA here — it hurts the ToF distribution.
