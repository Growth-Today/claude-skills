### GT high-performing words & phrases (add these to your rotation)

These are the words and phrases that consistently carry the GT voice — plain, spoken, verb-driven, and specific. Reach for them:

- **"here's exactly how"** — promises a concrete, followable system (not theory)
- **"the system that"** — frames work as a repeatable engine, not a one-off
- **"signal-based"** / **"signals"** — core GT positioning word for modern outbound
- **"compounding"** — the payoff word: results that stack over time
- **"allbound"** — GT's category term (content + outbound + ABM as one motion)
- **"scales output, not headcount"** — the one-line GT thesis
- **"forward-deployed"** — how GT frames hands-on delivery
- **"we ran this"** / **"we built this"** — proof-led openers (we did it, not we think it)
- **"here's what actually works"** — contrarian pivot into the real answer
- **"0 [X]"** — the zero-construction ("0 SDRs", "0 paid ads", "0 guessing") — high-contrast, very GT
- **"[number] → [number]"** — before/after transformation shorthand
- **"in the last 90 days"** — concrete, recent, credible timeframe
- **"without [the obvious painful thing]"** — reframes the win by what you avoided

**Say it like you talk.** Short words over long ones. Verbs over nouns. Talk TO the reader, not AT them. No em dashes, no corporate filler, no hype adjectives — the number and the specificity carry the weight.

## 3. SENTENCE STRUCTURE DNA

### Length Distribution (from top 25% posts)

- **All lines:** median 7 words, mean 9.2 words
- **Hook lines:** median 9 words, mean 10.3 words
- **Body lines:** median 7 words, mean 8.9 words
- **Sweet spot:** 4-14 words per line. 75% of all lines fall in this range.
- **Key insight:** Lines are SHORT. Median is 7 words. This is not essay writing — it's punchy, scannable content.

### Formatting Usage (% of top posts that use each)

- **P.S. section:** 63% — Most common structural element. Almost 2/3 of top posts have one.
- **Numbered steps (1️⃣, Step 1, etc.):** 56%
- **Arrows (↳→):** 43%
- **Emojis:** 37% — But sparingly (0-2 per post)
- **Bold unicode (𝗯𝗼𝗹𝗱):** 14%
