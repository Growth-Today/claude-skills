# Meta Ads Creative Formats (B2B)

Match format to funnel stage and placement. Mobile-first and sound-off are non-negotiable: 85% watch without sound, so captions and vertical/square framing are baseline.

## Format by funnel stage

| Stage | Best formats | Why |
|-------|-------------|-----|
| TOF (awareness) | 15-30s video, Reels (9:16) | Video carries awareness; Reels is highest-reach in 2026. Hook in 3s. |
| MOF (consideration) | Carousel, video, lead-form ads | Carousel shows features/use-cases/logos; carousel + lead form lifts qualified leads. |
| BOF (conversion) | 6-15s testimonial / demo video, single image with direct CTA | Short proof-led creative with a strong CTA converts warm traffic. |

## Specs

- **Feed (FB/IG):** 1:1 square or 4:5 vertical; 15-30s video with captions.
- **Stories / Reels:** 9:16 vertical, full-bleed, 1080x1920; 15-60s; hook in first 3 seconds.
- **Carousel:** 3-5+ cards; one idea per card (feature, use-case, client logo, step).
- **Single image:** clean, one message, direct CTA; test against video (GT finding: static can beat motion on some profiles).

## B2B creative types that work

- Founder / senior-team talking head (authenticity beats production value for trust).
- Screen recording / product walkthrough (strong for SaaS).
- Client testimonial naming the specific result, not generic praise.
- Problem walkthrough: describe a recognisable scenario, then pivot to the solution.
- Logo / social-proof carousel (recognisable companies reduce risk).
- Risk-reduction messaging: free consultation, no obligation, guarantee.

## Hook patterns (first 3 seconds)

- Name the pain ("Wasting budget on leads sales ignores?").
- Pattern interrupt (unexpected visual or statement).
- Social proof up front (rating, recognisable logos, a number).
- Direct question to the specific role you target.

## Production notes

- Clean audio + good lighting + a specific script beats glossy generic content for B2B.
- Captions always (sound-off viewing).
- Keep one clear message and one CTA per creative.
- Build a modular library so hooks/bodies/CTAs can be recombined fast for weekly testing.

## What to avoid

- Audience Network placement for B2B lead gen.
- Over-polished brand films at the bottom of the funnel (proof converts better than production).
- Reusing the same creative for cold and warm audiences (match the message to temperature).
- Assuming motion always wins; test static vs video per account and offer.
