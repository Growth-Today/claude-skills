# Meta Ads 2026 — AI Campaigns, Creative & Placements

The 2026 Advantage+/AI updates that change how a B2B account is run. Load alongside `campaign-setup.md`, `creative.md`, and `learning-phase.md`. **Evidence discipline:** the performance figures below are Meta-/vendor-reported — validate against your own before/after, don't promise them.

---

## Opportunity Score
Ads Manager now shows an **Opportunity Score (0–100)** rating your account setup across audience breadth, creative diversity, budget allocation, conversion tracking, and Advantage+ adoption. Higher scores correlate (vendor-reported) with lower CPA and steadier delivery.

- Use it as a **triage signal**, not a target — a high score with bad lead quality is still a bad account. Read the specific recommendations, apply the ones that fit a B2B motion (esp. tracking + creative diversity), ignore the ones that push broad B2C-style targeting where ICP precision matters.

## Advantage+ 2026 updates
- **Lower conversion threshold** — Advantage+ campaigns now need ~**25 conversions/week** (down from higher floors), so more B2B accounts can actually use them.
- **Predictive Budget Allocation** — automatically shifts spend to high-performing segments in real time (early tests: ~8–15% better ROAS).
- Advantage+ reportedly delivers ~**22% higher ROAS** than manual on average — but on B2B, judge on **pipeline/SQL**, not ROAS, and keep manual control where ICP precision or ABM matters.

## Advantage+ Creative Enhancements (generative AI)
Meta can crop, animate statics, swap backgrounds, generate text variations, turn stills into video, and allocate budget to AI-generated creative tests.

- Use for **variant volume** to outrun fatigue (see `creative-fatigue.md`) and quick first drafts — but **human-review every variant** before publish, strip unverifiable claims, and keep it on-brand. AI variants supplement the testing plan; they don't replace hooks/offer strategy.

## Advantage+ Leads
Advantage+ applied to lead-gen; Meta reports ~**10% lower cost per qualified lead**. Relevant for B2B, but the usual rule holds: sync leads to CRM and judge on SQL/pipeline, not raw CPL (see `crm-attribution.md`).

## Threads placements
Threads ads are broadly available (~400M MAU). Treat as an **experimental placement** — test in isolation, watch lead quality closely for B2B before allocating meaningful budget; don't let it ride inside Advantage+ placements unmonitored.
