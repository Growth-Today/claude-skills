# Key Benchmarks & Metrics

## Click-Through Rates (CTR)

Always benchmark against the specific ad format. The blended 0.44-0.65% sponsored-content number hides huge format-level variation.

| Type | Benchmark |
|------|-----------|
| Standard image ad | 0.42-1% |
| Personalized ABM ad | 5-10% |
| Minimum acceptable (Sponsored Content) | 0.45% |
| Document Ads | 0.43% median (6-7%+ achievable when strong) |
| Thought Leader Ads organic predictor | 2-3% organic → top ad performer |

### Per-format CTR / CPC (ZenABM 2026 — median across 161,256 ads / 211 companies)

| Format | Median CTR | Median CPC | LP clicks per $1K |
|--------|-----------|-----------|-------------------|
| Thought Leader Ads | 2.68% | $2.29 | 327 |
| Event Ads | 0.55% | - | - |
| Document Ads | 0.43% | - | - |
| Single Image Ads | 0.42% | $13.23 | 71 |
| Carousel Ads | 0.32% | $13.30 | - |
| Video Ads | 0.24% | $15.61 | 62 |
| Dynamic/Spotlight | 0.08% | - | - |
| Text Ads | 0.02% | - | - |

### CTR by geography (Sponsored Content, ZenABM 2026)

| Geography | Median CTR |
|-----------|-----------|
| Netherlands | 0.72% |
| UK | 0.55% |
| US | 0.52% |
| Global average | 0.44-0.65% |

### Dwell time by format

- Thought Leader Ads: 6.63 sec
- Carousel Ads: 4.56 sec
- Single Image Ads: 3.64 sec

### "Good" CTR by campaign purpose

| Purpose | Best format | "Good" CTR | Measure instead |
|---------|------------|-----------|-----------------|
| Awareness / ToF | Thought Leader Ads | 2.0%+ | dwell time, engagement |
| Mid-funnel | Single Image | 0.4%+ | landing page conversion |
| Education / nurture | Video | 0.24%+ | view / completion rate |
| Storytelling | Carousel | 0.3%+ | card progression, dwell |
| Retargeting / deanon | Text Ads | n/a | CPM (~$2) |

**CTR does not predict pipeline.** Across 211 companies, CTR vs pipeline correlated negatively (Spearman rho = -0.170). High-CTR formats attract broad engagement including non-buyers; lower-CTR formats often carry stronger intent. Optimise for pipeline and targeting, not clicks. Best accounts use a mix and measure attribution at the pipeline level.

### Cost proof points (third-party, for client decks)

- **Metadata experiment:** TLA CPC $4.14 vs $22.54 for brand-awareness image/video campaigns. Different dataset from ZenABM, same conclusion — TLAs are dramatically cheaper per click.
- **Channel weight:** LinkedIn carries ~41% of total B2B ad budgets and drives ~80% of B2B social leads. CPCs run high ($6-10) and CPMs are higher than other paid social, but the targeting precision is why the economics still clear.
- **Spend floor to learn:** most B2B teams need ~$3,000-5,000/month in spend to test multiple audiences and offers per funnel stage; expect a 2-3 month learning phase and 3-6 months to read true pipeline impact.

## Campaign Health Indicators

**Healthy campaign**:
- CTR > 0.45% (Sponsored Content)
- Spending 80%+ of daily budget
- Demographics tab shows ICP alignment
- Frequency < 3 impressions/week/person
- Retargeting pool growing over time

**Needs attention**:
- CTR < 0.45%
- Under-spending budget consistently
- Demographics show off-target audience
- Declining engagement over 2+ weeks
- No pipeline influence after 3 months

## Attribution Reality
- Only 20-30% of LinkedIn conversions captured
- 90%+ impact occurs without clickthrough
- 83% impressions on mobile, 72% conversions on desktop
- Average B2B deal involves 6-10 decision-makers
