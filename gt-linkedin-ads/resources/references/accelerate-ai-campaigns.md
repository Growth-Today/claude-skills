# Accelerate AI Campaigns & AI Creative (2026)

LinkedIn's AI-driven campaign and creative tooling — what it does, where it helps, and where a B2B advertiser should keep manual control. Load alongside `campaign-setup.md` and `creative-strategy.md`.

## What Accelerate is

**Accelerate** is LinkedIn's AI-built campaign mode. You give it your product/service/brand info (and a URL), and it generates a near-complete campaign: **AI-generated ad variants, suggested audience targets, placements, and automatic ongoing optimization.** It is now **generally available for Lead Generation and Website Visits** objectives, and has expanded from single-image only to also support **video and document ads**.

LinkedIn's own claim: Accelerate delivers roughly **42% better cost-per-action than classic (manual) campaigns.** Treat that as a vendor claim, not a guarantee — it depends heavily on seed inputs, creative quality, and how tightly you constrain the audience.

### Where Accelerate genuinely helps

- **Lean teams / limited setup time** — it removes hours of manual campaign construction.
- **Lower-funnel, high-volume objectives** (website visits, straightforward lead gen) where breadth is acceptable.
- **A fast baseline** to benchmark your hand-built campaigns against.

### Where to keep manual control

- **ABM and named-account targeting.** Accelerate's audience suggestions broaden reach; that fights account-list precision. For ABM, build campaigns manually (see `abm/linkedin-ads-abm-guide.md`).
- **Tight ICP / high-CPL enterprise motions** where every impression must stay inside a narrow persona.
- **Brand-sensitive creative.** Review every AI-generated variant before it runs — AI copy can drift off-message or over-promise.

**GT default:** use Accelerate as a *challenger*, not the *system of record*. Run it against a manually structured campaign, compare pipeline (not CTR), and keep whichever produces qualified pipeline per dollar.

## AI creative tools

LinkedIn's creative AI can generate **new headlines, introductory text, and image concepts** from a single seed input ("AI Ad Variants"), drawing on your existing ad content, generative AI, and the **Shutterstock** library for visuals.

**Use it for:**
- Rapid **variant generation** to beat creative fatigue (see `creative.md` fatigue rules) — spin 4–6 on-brand variants instead of writing each by hand.
- First-draft **headline/intro** options you then edit to GT voice.

**Guardrails (non-negotiable):**
- **Human review before publish.** AI copy tends toward generic benefit-speak; tighten to a specific claim or number.
- **No unverifiable claims.** Strip any AI-inserted stat you can't source.
- **Keep the link placement / format discipline** the human rules require (e.g. TLA link in the bottom 25% of the post).
- AI variants supplement the creative testing plan; they don't replace a deliberate hook/offer strategy.

## Real-time CRM data in Campaign Manager

Since **June 2025**, LinkedIn surfaces **real-time CRM data directly inside Campaign Manager**, so you can see how campaigns influence pipeline and revenue without exporting to a spreadsheet. This pairs with the Conversions API and the Revenue Attribution Report (see `measurement-attribution.md`) to close the "clicks vs pipeline" gap that has always plagued LinkedIn reporting. Connect the CRM early so optimization decisions are made on pipeline, not clicks.
